Welcome to Lunar Latte Cafe

Nestled in the heart of Lagos, Lunar Latte is more than just a coffee shop, it's a space designed for connection, comfort, and exceptional flavors. Our mission is to serve expertly crafted coffee made from ethically sourced beans, paired with a selection of fresh, handcrafted pastries.
